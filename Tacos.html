My Favorites

In my opinion, these are the best taco shops in all of existence. They are packed with flavor and are all around good quality Tacos. Taco Bell is the one exception as it is very Americanized but I still like it.

  1. Tito's Tacos
    • Culver City, CA
  2. Backyard Taco
    • Mesa, AZ
  3. Olas
    • Camarillo, CA
  4. Taco Bell
    • Anywhere in the USA
